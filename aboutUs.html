About Project

We are Group 4 of the University of South Florida's Fall 2022 Distributed Information System Course. At Muma College of Business, we are pursuing an MS in Business Analytics & Information Systems. The goal of this project is to give us practice with HTML, CSS, and, if desired, JavaScript to produce high-quality design outcomes. The project is accessible at(Github link),Please see our contact information below.


Project Description

This website acts as a conduit for USF students who are actively looking for employment or internships to look for positions, manage their online portfolios, and stay up to date with events, all of which assist in the employment of USF graduates.
